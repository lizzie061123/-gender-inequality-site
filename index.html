About Gender Inequality

Gender inequality refers to the unequal treatment or perceptions of individuals based on their gender. It impacts access to education, employment, healthcare, and leadership opportunities.

Key Statistics

  • Women earn 77 cents for every dollar men earn.
  • Only 25% of global leadership roles are held by women.
  • 132 million girls worldwide are out of school.
